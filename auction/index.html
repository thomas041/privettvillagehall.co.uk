Payment - latest news

At the moment, the most favourable methods of payment will be Cash or Cheque when accompanied by some form of identification with an address.

We have obtained a card reading device, but this has proved to be very slow and could be unreliable. Cash and cheques will still be preferable, however it is possible that we will be able to accept debit cards.

Commission Bids

If you cannot attend the auction in person to bid you can submit a commission bid.
Commission bids are the maximum amount up to which the auctioneer may bid on your behalf. They will be executed at the lowest possible price with regard to reserve and competing bids. Commission bids may now only be made on the day using the form in the catalogue.

Please arrange collection by 6pm on the day where possible. 
If you are not able to arrange collection on the day please email further details re your plans for collection to make sure these are acceptable.

Payment should be made by cash on collection.
